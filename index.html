About Me

Hi, I'm Oliveira Hambaena, a 20-year-old software development student in my 3rd year at the Namibia University of Science and Technology, based in Windhoek, Namibia. My passion for technology started at a young age when I would explore every setting and feature of any electronic device I could get my hands on. This curiosity grew into a love for computers, leading me to develop skills in hardware repair, software troubleshooting, and operating system optimization — a field I aim to specialize in one day.

Over time, I turned my passion into a source of income by offering repair and support services for laptops, computers, and other hardware. Beyond technical problem-solving, I’m deeply enthusiastic about VFX, game design, and animation. My creativity extends to graphic design, where I believe I have a natural talent for crafting visually appealing designs. I’m always eager to push my limits by learning new programming languages, mastering VFX, and exploring deeper concepts in graphic design.

What sets me apart is my ability to connect with people. I enjoy collaborating with like-minded individuals who share the same goals and vision. I’m not the "typical computer geek" stereotype — I value time away from screens to gain new perspectives, which ultimately allows me to create work that is meaningful to people, not just impressive to tech enthusiasts. My dream is to one day work on operating system development, or in any field where I can merge creativity with technical problem-solving.
